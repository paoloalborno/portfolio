About Me

A software engineer with a passion for building complex solutions.

My Mission

I'm Paolo Alborno, a Software Engineer with solid experience in the world of cloud computing, particularly with AWS, and in backend development. My mission is to design and build the architecture behind the scenes, ensuring that applications are high-performing, scalable, and secure.

I hold AWS, Scrum, and Project Management certifications, which allow me to approach projects with a holistic view, combining technical expertise with effective management of the development process. I have worked on projects ranging from analyzing e-commerce infrastructures to developing Natural Language Processing (NLP) models with BERT, demonstrating versatility and strong problem-solving skills.

I firmly believe in the power of technology to solve real-world problems and I am always looking for new challenges to refine my skills and contribute to innovative projects.
